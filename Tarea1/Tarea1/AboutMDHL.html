General Information

The Mighty Ducks Hockey League was established in 1996 to provide athletesresiding in Chicago's northside neighborhoods an environment in which to learn and play hockey. To be a member of MDHL, you must be between the ages of 4-12 and reside in a Chicago northside neighborhood. MDHL is ran by a small fulltime staff, and relies on the generous volunteer time of parents and previous league members.
